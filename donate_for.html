Human Welfare, Extending a Helping Hand for a Better Tomorrow

Human Welfare at its core is about 'A Circle of Care,' where we empower humanity with dignity. Our mission extends across various spheres of need, including education, food relief, disaster response, caring for orphans, and fostering unity through mass marriages. We strive to make a profound impact within the boundaries of Shariah, guided by the well-known hadith that proclaims, 'The most beloved of people to Allah are those who are most beneficial to people,' and 'Whoever among you is able to benefit his brother, he should do so.' Through our initiatives, we aim to foster a world where individuals and groups have the tools they need to thrive, and where compassion and support are the cornerstones of our shared humanity.

Animal Aid, Nurturing Hope, One Paw at a Time

At Animal Aid, our mission transcends more than just providing care to our furry friends; it's about Saving Lives and Uplifting Spirits. We believe in a world where every animal, no matter how small or vulnerable, deserves compassion, protection, and a sense of belonging. Our dedicated team tirelessly rescues and rehabilitates animals in need, while also promoting responsible pet ownership and raising crucial awareness about animal welfare. We aspire to make a profound difference within the bounds of Shariah, inspired by the teachings of Islam that emphasize kindness to animals. As a well-known hadith states, 'Fear Allah in your treatment of animals.' Your support for Animal Aid ensures that every stray animal is treated with love, care, and kindness, as we work diligently to create a world where no animal is left behind.
